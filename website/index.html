Why OpenSSH?

Everything you need to manage servers from your phone.

No Port Forwarding

Your PC connects outward. No firewall rules, no dynamic DNS, no router config needed.

Full Color Terminal

ANSI 256-color support. Run vim, htop, colored ls — everything looks right on mobile.

File Browser

Browse, view, and transfer files between your phone and PC with a native iOS-style interface.

SSH Key Pinning

TOFU key verification prevents man-in-the-middle attacks. Keys persist across relay restarts.

Auto PTY Resize

Rotate your phone or resize the window — the remote terminal adapts instantly.

Auto Reconnect

Network drops? No problem. Both the host daemon and the app reconnect automatically.

Download the app & create an account

Install the OpenSSH app on your phone. Register with your email and the relay URL.

Install the host daemon on your PC
